Our Story

Bringing Bali to Your Mauritian Home

At Cosy & Comfy, we believe your home should be your happiest place. Born from a love of bohemian aesthetics and Balinese craftsmanship, we curate a unique collection of natural-fiber decor that brings warmth, texture, and soul to every corner.

Every rug is hand-woven with real natural jute and seagrass sourced from Bali. Every rattan piece is crafted by skilled artisans. Every basket, pouf, and cachepot is chosen because we'd proudly put it in our own homes.

Now located in Grand Baie, we're proud to serve all of Mauritius with competitive prices and exceptional personal service.
